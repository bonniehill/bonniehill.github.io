Lukewarm is a 1997 e-zine turned physical publication. In this comparative version, Twitter posts have been placed in conjunction with the short stories to show the similarities in obscure humour between 1997 and 2019. Many of the stories in this zine hold sarcastic and dark undertones and have been paired with a selection of "meme aesthetic" images from the internet that loosely complement each zine issue.
